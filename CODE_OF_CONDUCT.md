# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in OpenGen a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project
- Showing empathy toward other community members

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Deliberately submitting false reviews to manipulate the knowledge graph
- Coordinated voting campaigns intended to bias verification outcomes
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the founders directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct may face temporary or permanent repercussions.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces - GitHub, opengen.live, community discussions, and any other forum where OpenGen is represented.
